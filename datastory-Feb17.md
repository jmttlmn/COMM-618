# Take Five: Giddy Up
### Reuters
https://www.reuters.com/business/take-five/global-markets-themes-graphic-2026-02-13/

This article is a collection of mini-stories from the past few weeks relating to business and how they'll impact the space going forward. This is basically supposed to operate as a quick way to consume info about each of these topics, and for that purpose, I think it does a good job. However, I would've liked to see more than just one graph for each issue. I know this is supposed to be a short bit about each, but some of the topics definitely felt like they needed more analysis to understand.
